What is ReadySetRecruit?

Ready Set Recruit is a recruitment automation platform I built to streamline the networking process for students pursuing highly competitive careers like Investment Banking and Consulting. Instead of spending hours every day sending cold networking emails with extremely low response rates (<1%), students can focus on preparing for interviews, technicals, and casing.

The goal is to level the playing field for non-target students, reducing the manual networking burden and giving them more time to sharpen the skills that actually secure offers.
