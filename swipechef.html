SwipeChef

SwipeChef is a meal-prep platform designed to make cooking easier by automating recipe discovery and grocery planning. Think of it as “Meal Prep on Steroids.”

Features include:

  • Smart Recipe Matching – recommends meals based on preferences and pantry items
  • One-Click Grocery List – automatically generates and organizes ingredients
  • Scalable Tech – built with AI integrations and mobile-first design
