What is SwipeChef?

SwipeChef is an innovative recipe discovery and meal-planning platform designed to make cooking both effortless and engaging. Inspired by the swipe-based simplicity of dating apps, SwipeChef allows users to explore thousands of curated recipes by simply swiping left or right, creating a seamless and interactive browsing experience. Once a recipe is selected, the app automatically generates a comprehensive ingredient list and integrates with local supermarkets for instant delivery or easy pickup.

Step-by-step cooking instructions, smart dietary filters, and personalized recommendations help users save time, reduce food waste, and feel confident in the kitchen. By transforming meal discovery into a playful, frictionless experience, SwipeChef reimagines how people connect with food.
